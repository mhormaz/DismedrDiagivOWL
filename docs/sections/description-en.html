DismedrDiagiv: Description back to ToC

The DismedrDiagiv ontology models the European catalog of high-risk medical devices, as well as in vitro diagnostic devices, which fall under the regulatory framework of the European MDR (EU 2017/745) and IVDR (EU 2017/746) Regulations. In this way, the most important data in this domain will be represented in a structured manner, including the devices, their types, manufacturers, incidents and their risks, as well as corrective actions in response to them.
