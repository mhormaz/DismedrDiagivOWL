Introduction back to ToC

The purpose of this model is to create and share a model that allows consulting, adding, or modifying information about all high-risk medical devices and in vitro diagnostic devices that are under the European regulatory framework (MDR and IVDR).
Its main use would be for health authorities, who could consult information about the medical devices they use, which could serve not only to report problems (manufacturer information is available) but also to check their status, so that in case of a problem, measures can be taken against malfunctions (if they have harmful defects for users). At the same time, it could also serve as a catalog for purchasing equipment.
